Our goal

The aim of Energym is to promote a more sustainable way of working out. Where others take, make and dispose, we generate, convert and reuse. We want to set a new standard of carbon neutrality in the fitness industry.

The founder

HEC Paris graduate, Agathe Martin decided to build this gym after noticing how much energy was needed to power one single gym. We should not have to compromise the Earth's health for our own.
